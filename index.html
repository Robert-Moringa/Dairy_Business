ABOUT US...

Maina Firm is a dairy firm located olong the riftvalley in Nyandarua County. The farm has a population of 48 cows and 19 calves. We provide best quality milk since our cows feed on the green vegetatition sprouting from the fertile Aberdare soil. We have specialist that everyday work to ensure production of quality milk; hygiene and all standards considered. Brookside Diaries are our main patners that we supply our milk.
